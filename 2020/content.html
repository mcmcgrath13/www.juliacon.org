JuliaCon

JuliaCon is the biggest Julia conference of the year, bringing together speakers, developers, and enthusiasts from all over the world, for 5 days packed with keynotes, talks and workshops! Videos of last year's event are available online, and a highlight is presented below.

Sponsor

JuliaCon could not be possible without the help and support of our sponsors. Please take a look at our sponsor page if you or your organisation would be willing to sponsor the conference. JuliaCon is the best place to meet an engaged audience of numerical programmers and scientists.

Venue

The conference talks, workshops and hackathon will be held at the campus of ISCTE - Instituto Universitário de Lisboa (ISCTE-IUL) at Avenida das Forças Armadas, 1649-026 Lisbon, Portugal.

Accommodation

Lisbon is a popular tourist city, and has a wide range of accomodation to choose from, for all budgets. A few options are mentioned on our accommodation page. Our event takes place in the summer, which is a busy season, so please book early to get the best prices.

Code of Conduct

JuliaCon is dedicated to providing a positive conference experience for all attendees, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, age, religion, or national and ethnic origin. We encourage respectful and considerate interactions between attendees and do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. For example, offensive or sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any conference venue, including formal talks and networking between sessions. Conference participants violating these standards may be sanctioned or expelled from the conference (without a refund) at the discretion of the conference organizers. Our anti-harassment policy and information about who to contact if you have an issue you'd like to discuss can be found here.

Committee

JuliaCon is organized by a committee composed entirely of volunteers, and can be reached at juliacon@julialang.org with any questions or comments.

Financial Assistance

Apply for financial assistance by March 7, 2020 here! We offer financial aid to both speakers and Julia users from underrepresented groups. While we aim to satisfy as many requests as possible, our budget is limited — primarily by the amount of sponsorship we can raise.
